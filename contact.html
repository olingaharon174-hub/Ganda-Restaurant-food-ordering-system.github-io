Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer delivery services within a 10km radius of our restaurant. You can order online through our website or call us directly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, mobile money (MTN, Airtel Money), and credit/debit cards for online orders.

Do you cater for events?

Yes, we provide catering services for events, parties, and corporate functions. Please contact us for more details and pricing.

Can I make reservations?

While we don't require reservations, you can call ahead to reserve a table, especially during peak hours or for large groups.
